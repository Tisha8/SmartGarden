📲 Telegram Bot Setup (Arduino R4)

1
Open Telegram → search @BotFather → type /newbot → get your BOT_TOKEN
2
Search @userinfobot on Telegram → get your CHAT_ID
3
In Arduino code: fill in BOT_TOKEN, CHAT_ID, and your WiFi SSID + PASSWORD
4
Install library: UniversalTelegramBot by Brian Lough in Arduino IDE Library Manager
5
Upload the Arduino sketch → alerts will arrive on your phone when soil is dry or tank is low ✅
